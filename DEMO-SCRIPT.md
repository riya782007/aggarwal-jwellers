   - Scroll: hero, trust badges, **Shop by Category**, **Bestsellers** with real star ratings, festive banner, **Happy Customers** testimonials.

3. **Product page** → open any piece (e.g. Rajwadi Kundan Necklace Set)

4. **Diva, the AI concierge** → click the ✦ button (bottom-right)
   - Ask: _"show me kundan earrings under ₹500"_ or _"what's good for a wedding?"_
   - _"A live AI assistant that knows the catalogue, prices, stock, shipping and returns. The old site has nothing like this."_

5. **Wishlist + Cart + Checkout**
